Chatbot Arena

Chatbot Arena is an open-source platform for evaluating large language models by human preference, developed by researchers from UC Berkeley SkyLab and LMSYS. Join us at lmarena.ai to vote for your top models and contribute to the live leaderboard!

We welcome contributions from the community. If you’re interested in getting involved, we’d love to hear from you!
